Feteer is a flaky, buttery Egyptian pastry that can be enjoyed plain or stuffed with sweet or savory fillings.
Escalope is crispy, breaded meat cutlet, served with fries and a side of garlic sauce or salad.
Macaroni Bashamel is a delicious baked pasta dish layered with seasoned ground meat and creamy béchamel sauce.
